Make It Happen Club (SCIO) is a charity with the aim to inspire childrens digital learning! We run an innovative and exciting App Design competition for entire Primary Schools across Scotland.

Why?

We intend to enable pupils to think, design and (if they win) build an application with the help of our expert engineers.

Our goal is to reach all pupils from P1-P7 and let them start their digital design thinking.




Who?

We are working with Primary Schools across Scotland to allow us to inspire and excite future engineers!

We include all pupils from P1-P7 in the primary schools.
